Step 2 of 3: Organization details

Choose your plan based on the number of electronic sigantures needed for your organisation

Functionality: Each plan includes electronic signing, secure business collaboration, task management and file sharing. Enterprise plan also includes an unlimited number of API integrations.

Reasonable use: Your subscription includes the right to use all functionality included in the selected license plan as long as your use of the service can be considered to be within the scope of customary and reasonable use taking into consideration your paid monthly fees. Use of the service is not customary and reasonable, for example in cases where customer is using in large scale messages or chat functionalities instead of electronic signatures or when the number of customer invited and strongly identified participants in any cooperation areas in substantially higher than the number of electronic signatures made in the service.
