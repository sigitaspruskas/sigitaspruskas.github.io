RRetention period is tracked for each file and chat activity separately from the point in time when it is first created.

After the indicated retention period each content is automatically removed from your account. Content may remain visible to other users invited in the chat depending on their retention settings. Content will be permanently deleted from SignSpace once no user has access to it.

Maximum retention for chat content is 12 months.

New functionality is being developed which will allow more control over the retention periods.
