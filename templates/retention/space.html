Retention period is tracked for each file and space activity (message, signing request, task, poll) separately from the point in time when it is first created.

After the indicated retention period each content is automatically removed from your account. ontent may remain visible to other users invited in the space depending on their retention settings. Content will be permanently deleted from SignSpace once no user has access to it.

Maximum retention for files signed in spaces is 10 years. Maximum retention for other space content is 12 months.

New functionality is being developed which will allow more control over the retention periods.
